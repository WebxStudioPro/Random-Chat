Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

1. Introduction

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how our Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Random Chat network ("We", "Us", "Our") handles, processes, and protects your information when you access our platform. Because our architecture differs significantly from traditional server-client chat applications, our data practices are inherently designed to maximize user anonymity.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect or Store

To ensure total privacy and anonymity, our platform utilizes direct WebRTC technology. Because data travels directly between you and the connected peer, we explicitly declare that:

  • No Chat Logs: We do not monitor, intercept, or store any text messages exchanged during a session.
  • No Media Storage: Audio recordings, voice notes, and images sent over the data channel are never uploaded to our servers.
  • No Personal Accounts: We do not require or store emails, passwords, phone numbers, or real identities. The "Nickname" you choose is temporary and exists only within your browser.

Once a P2P session is disconnected, all memory associated with that connection is permanently flushed and destroyed.

3. Data Processed for Matchmaking (Signaling)

To function as a matching engine, our backend server temporarily processes minimal routing data. This process is fully automated:

  • IP Addresses & ICE Candidates: To establish a WebRTC connection, your IP address is temporarily processed by our signaling server to introduce you to a stranger. This routing data is standard internet protocol and is not logged or stored permanently.
  • System Radar: We use automated network radar scripts (via MQTT) strictly for counting active users and identifying malicious network abuse (e.g., bot spamming). Abusive IP addresses may be blocked by our firewall to maintain network integrity.

4. Third-Party Advertising and Cookies

To keep the platform 100% free and sustain our high-speed servers, we partner with third-party advertising networks (e.g., Adsterra). Please be aware of the following regarding ad networks:

These third-party vendors may use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect non-personal data (such as your browser type, OS, generalized geographic location, and interaction with ads) to provide targeted advertising.

We do not have access to or control over these third-party cookies. The privacy practices of these advertisers are governed by their respective privacy policies. You can configure your browser settings to reject cookies if you prefer not to participate in targeted advertising.

5. Peer-to-Peer Risks (Security Disclaimer)

While WebRTC provides End-to-End Encryption (DTLS and SRTP protocols) for the data traveling between peers, connecting directly to another user inherently means your IP address is exposed to the other peer's device (not our server, but the stranger's device). This is a standard characteristic of all P2P networks. By using this service, you accept this technological reality.
