Safe Chatting Guide & Community Rules

Effective Date: February 2026

1. The True P2P Reality (Your Ultimate Privacy)

Unlike traditional chat platforms that store your data on massive servers, our engine is built on true WebRTC Peer-to-Peer (P2P) architecture. This fundamentally changes how your privacy works.

WE CANNOT READ YOUR CHATS:
Because your connection is a direct, decentralized tunnel to the stranger, we literally do not have the technical ability to read your messages, view your photos, or listen to your voice notes. There is no central database recording your conversation. Whatever is shared in the private room is strictly between the two connected strangers and disappears forever once the connection is closed.

3. Strict Community Guidelines

Although we do not monitor private chat rooms, our network radar monitors the overall health of the matchmaking lobby. Engaging in any of the following activities is strictly prohibited:

  • Distributing or requesting illegal content, including material involving minors or extreme real-life violence.
  • Using automated bots, scripts, or emulators to spam the matchmaking engine.
  • Attempting to DDoS, hack, or overload our signaling server infrastructure.
  • Impersonating minors or attempting to use this platform if you are under 18 years of age.

4. Enforcement: Permanent IP & Device Bans

NO ACCOUNTS, ONLY HARD BANS: Because our platform is 100% anonymous and does not use user accounts, we do not issue "account suspensions." Instead, our automated security protocols (Admin Radar) detect malicious network behavior. Violators will face an immediate, permanent ban at the IP and Device level.

If you are banned by the firewall, you will lose all access to the matchmaking lobby indefinitely. We do not accept appeals for bans related to illegal activities or network abuse.

5. Parental Responsibility (18+ Strictly Enforced)

This platform is exclusively for adults (18+). It is the responsibility of parents and legal guardians to ensure their children do not access adult-oriented platforms. We strongly recommend using device-level Parental Controls (such as Google Family Link or Apple Screen Time) to block access to this website if a minor uses your device.
